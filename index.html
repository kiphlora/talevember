Welcome!

Talevember is an alternative to, or maybe even a method to accomplish, NaNoWriMo.

It's inspired by Inktober and involves writing (generally short) fiction or essays based on prompts each day.

For 2025, the aim is to train our imaginations to co-create our futures.
